Model Assumptions
  • Your donation immediately unlocks 35% in debt financing — deployed alongside your gift into new infrastructure
  • New households contribute a $100 customer deposit in year one, $80 in subsequent years — further expanding the HH base
  • Household connection cost: $400
  • Average household size: 9.7 people
  • 20% of annual sales revenue is retained and reinvested exclusively into new household connections — not O&M or debt service
  • Retained earnings grow at 2% per year after the donation period ends
  • Impact is measured as new water access only — households funded by immediate leverage plus retained earnings reinvestment. O&M costs and debt service are excluded.
  • A lump-sum gift reaches 1.7× impact immediately from leverage alone, and doubles new access within 5 years as retained earnings compound
  • Multi-year gifts spread deployment across cohorts — each cohort follows the same doubling curve independently
